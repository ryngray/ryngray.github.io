#Switch dancing

Switch dancing (for me) incompases everything that is not traditional leading and following.  Often, this involves switching who is leading and who is following in a dance.  But I believe this can also include things such as no one leading/following, power following, and more.  

I currently teach switch dancing concepts and love to talk about how to get folks interested in switch dancing.  Please reach out if you are curious on what this looks like for a tiny scene.

I am a judge the Open Role Contest at Camp Hollywood, beginning in 2023.  I also helped organize and run the Switch-A-Room at Camp Hollywood also beginning in 2023.  Follow [@the\_jelly\_roles](https://www.instagram.com/the_jelly_roles/) on Instagram for video of this.
