# Fast Foot Fried Chicken Friday

The inaugural Fast Foot Fried Chicken Friday is happening May 3rd at the Dragon House!

An evening to hang out, dance your fastest and eat some tasty food


# What to expect

We will play faster songs than you'll hear at many of the dances around town.  Expect balboa and collegiate shag and maybe some fast lindy!  We're happy to show you the basics of any of these if you're new to them.


We have a deep fryer and will make the best fried chicken in Santa Fe. Please feel welcome to bring a side dishor dessert or drink.

# Where is Dragon House? 

Find or message Brian or Ryn and they'll get you the address.
